Stream Information & Career Paths

Explore the different academic streams and their potential career outcomes

Science

Ideal for students interested in mathematics, physics, chemistry, and biology. Leads to careers in engineering, medicine, research, and technology.

Career Options:

  • Engineer
  • Doctor
  • Scientist
  • IT Professional

Famous Personalities:

  • A.P.J. Abdul Kalam
  • C.V. Raman
  • Sundar Pichai

Commerce

Perfect for students with an aptitude for business, economics, and mathematics. Opens doors to careers in finance, business management, and entrepreneurship.

Career Options:

  • Chartered Accountant
  • Business Manager
  • Economist
  • Banker

Famous Personalities:

  • Azim Premji
  • Kumar Mangalam Birla
  • Nirmala Sitharaman

Arts

Suited for students with a passion for literature, social sciences, and creative fields. Leads to careers in law, journalism, design, and social work.

Career Options:

  • Lawyer
  • Journalist
  • Designer
  • Social Worker

Famous Personalities:

  • Amartya Sen
  • Arundhati Roy
  • Kiran Bedi
