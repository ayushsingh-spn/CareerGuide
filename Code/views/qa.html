Frequently Asked Questions

How do I choose between Science and Commerce?

Consider your interests and strengths. If you enjoy mathematics and scientific concepts, Science might be a good fit. If you're interested in business and economics, Commerce could be the right choice. It's also helpful to think about your long-term career goals.

What careers can I pursue with an Arts stream?

The Arts stream opens up various career paths including journalism, law, teaching, social work, psychology, design, and many more. It's a versatile choice that allows you to develop critical thinking and creativity.

Is it possible to change streams after 12th grade?

Yes, it is possible to change streams after 12th grade, although it may require additional coursework or bridge programs depending on the field you want to enter. Many universities offer foundation courses to help students transition between streams.

How important are board exam results for my future career?

While board exam results are important for college admissions, they don't define your entire career. Many successful professionals didn't score the highest marks. Focus on learning concepts thoroughly and developing skills relevant to your chosen field.
