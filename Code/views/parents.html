Parental Guidance

Supporting your child in making informed career decisions

As a parent, you play a crucial role in your child's career decisions. Your guidance, support, and understanding can make a significant difference in their journey. Here are some resources to help you guide your child effectively.

Remember

  • Every child is unique and may have different interests and aptitudes.
  • Encourage exploration of various career paths.
  • Focus on your child's strengths and passions rather than societal pressures.
  • Stay informed about current educational and career trends.
  • Seek professional career counseling if needed.
